About NZG

Hey! I’m Ntsako Gomba — the founder of NZG Photography. I’m a passionate photographer and visual storyteller based in South Africa. My goal is simple: to help people feel seen, confident, and proud through beautiful, natural photos.

I shoot with my trusted Nikon D3000 and specialize in portraits, events, and lifestyle visuals. Over time, I’ve also expanded into stylish streetwear that reflects the creative energy of NZG.

📸 Journey So Far

  • 2020: Picked up a camera for the first time and fell in love with photography.
  • 2021: Practiced with friends and started doing free shoots to build experience.
  • 2022: Bought my Nikon D3000 and began shooting paid gigs.
  • 2023: Captured events, grew a loyal client base, and built my editing style.
  • 2024: Launched NZG Photography officially with merch & booking system.
  • 2025: Now offering nationwide shoots, merch drops, and creative projects.
